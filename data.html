Data release

You may obtain the dataset used in the shared task in the following two steps.

  1. Obtain the base corpus ICNALE from:
    https://language.sakura.ne.jp/icnale/download.html
  2. Send us (fcg-sharedtask[at]googlegroups.com) the signed term and condition of the dataset with a copy of the email sent from the ICNALE site when you obtained the ICNALE data.
